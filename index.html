Scanning Tips for Best Results:

  • Frame the entire tag: Make sure all text is visible, including the bottom barcode
  • Good lighting: Avoid shadows and ensure even lighting across the tag
  • Hold steady: Keep phone steady and parallel to the tag surface
  • Clear focus: Wait for the camera to focus clearly on the text
  • Distance: Hold phone about 20-30cm from the tag
